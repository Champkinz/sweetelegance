Amali Weerakoon, the eldest of the Weerakoon trio and founder of Sweet Elegance, currently resides in Auckland, New Zealand with her family. She has handed the reigns over to her sisters in order to continue the business.

The second sister, Sajani Abeysiriwardhana is happily married and resides in Kandy, Sri Lanka. She actively takes part in the baking process of the business while giving more attention to bulk cake orders.

The youngest of the three, Poornima Weerakoon is currently reading for her undergraduate degree in Colombo, Sri Lanka. She finds time to shuffle between her studies and manage the business.
